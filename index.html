What is matcha?

Matcha is powdered green tea leaves that is rich in antioxidants. It is typically used in Japananese tea ceremonies, but is enjoyed by many around the world. This tea has a unique, non-bitter taste and vibrant green color. It is considered the most aromatic form of green tea. The green tea powder is whisked into hot water, instead of steeped, to form a frothy drink.

Tools and ingrediants used to make matcha

  • Bamboo Matcha Whisk
  • Matcha Bowl
  • A high quality ceremonial grade matcha
  • A sweetener- to taste
  • Milk or alternative milk
  • Boiling Water
  • Ice- if you want an iced matcha

Exact products I use to make my personal matcha

  • Matcha Brand - Jade Leaf or Art of Tea (1 tbsp)
  • Sweetener - Agave or Honey (1 tsp)
  • Milk - Chobani Extra Creamy Oatmilk (1 cup)
  • Boiling water (1 cup)
  • Ice - I prefer an iced matcha
